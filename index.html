Our Love Story

We met in college during a mutual friend's birthday party. Michael spilled his drink on Sarah's dress, and instead of getting angry, she laughed it off. That moment sparked a friendship that grew into love.

After graduating, we moved to the same city and built our careers while supporting each other through every challenge. On a trip to Paris, Michael proposed at the Eiffel Tower at sunset.

Now, we're excited to begin our forever together, surrounded by the love and support of our family and friends.
